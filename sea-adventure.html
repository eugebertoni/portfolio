Sea Adventure

A mobile game to entertain yourself

What is Sea Adventure?

A conceptual game prototype to entertain players.

What makes Sea Adventure unique?

The game allows to play as different characters and get different results. The key object is to catch as many coins and gems as possible avoiding all obstacles.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Lead the product storyboarding and did light user research.
  • Did the User Experience Design including creating key user flows and wireframes.
  • Create high fidelity wireframes to show the full product experience.

Prototype to keep players entertained, motivated and relaxed

Storyboard

A storyboard help us identify key moments in the experience of a player.

  • Having free time

    Finally I have time for myself. I deserve some fun! Let's play a game.

  • Easy start

    I want to have fun now! Let's play a game.

  • Complete level

    I'm awesome! I've completed the first level, I want to continue.

  • Loses all lives

    Oh no! I was having so fun! I don't want to lose my progress.

  • Watch AD to continue

    OK, I'll watch the AD to continue playing.

  • EASY START
  • LEVEL COMPLETED
  • WATCH AD & GET A REWARD

The storyboard establish a vision with stakeholders and revealed issues and key screens early in the product development process.

How to make the game profitable?
What will motivate the player to watch an Ad?
What motivate users to buy extra coins?

The storyboard forced discussion with stakeholders, beyond the specifics of each screen.
This helped narrow the app scope before team invested time and money into the build.

Based on the storyboard, we identified the key tasks as well as the emotional state of the player at each stage of the game
